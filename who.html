How it started.

How it started.

We’re consumers just like you: constantly playing size roulette, seeking ways to carve out more time in the day and give back—all while trying to make more sustainable choices. After accumulating a “box of shame” filled with unwanted items that haven’t been returned, our founders Paul and Eliza Lin quickly realized that others share the same struggle and saw an opportunity to deliver a much needed solution to this growing problem.

The Returned.com app was born in 2023, delivering an innovative solution that leverages Paul’s technical savvy and Eliza’s product and operations expertise.

Where we’re headed.

A staggering amount of returns end up in landfills each year—something many consumers aren’t totally aware of. While some are shocked to learn this (we were too), we’re here to provide shoppers with a step in the right direction. We’ve partnered with 1% for the Planet and, for us, that’s just the beginning. We’re currently building a donation engine (integrated into our app) that allows users to easily donate their return. We’re all for finding ways to help you make a positive impact—stay tuned for so much more.
